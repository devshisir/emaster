Success Stories

A special co-curricular school, Realschool wanted to teach the most difficult age group, 6 to 10 online. Being an after school, they needed to be excellent in engaging students, how else would they attract more students. Another challenge was Realschool’s curriculum. They had workshops in STEM, Arts, Softs skills. Which highly complex interactions like hands on activities group games had to be done online and with the same efficacy as an offline workshop. With our program, Realschool today is successfully conducting workshops like Robotics, Bird watching, Star Gazing, 3d Printing, Animation, Theatre etc all online.

A coaching targeting Civil services wanted to move completely online with class strength going as large as 100 students. Emaester has worked closely with UPSC pathshala, trained and deployed over 100 online humanities trainers. We also developed mechanisms for their trainers to make student practice and learn subjective answer writing, which is practically impossible for offline coachings to achieve. It is currently handling student strength of upto 2000 enrollments per year with more than 300 hours of online training.
